Privacy Policy for SANTA SAVES XMAS.
Last Updated: 23rd December, 2025

Luminous Decibels ("we," "us," or "our") operates the 'Santa Saves Xmas' smartphone game.

1. Introduction
Our Privacy Policy is simple: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users. This game is designed to be a safe, fun, and private experience for players of all ages.

2. Data Collection
Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, phone number, or location).

Non-Personal Data: We do not collect technical data or device identifiers.

Local Storage: The game may save your progress (high scores, settings, gift counts) locally on your device using Hive/Local Storage. This data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

3. Advertising and Third Parties
No Ads: Our game does not display any third-party advertisements.

No Third-Party SDKs: We do not use any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Firebase) or tracking tools that monitor your behavior.

4. Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance)
Because our game is intended for "kids and old alike," we take children's privacy very seriously:

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Our app is fully compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Permissions
The app requires no special permissions (such as access to your Camera, Contacts, or Microphone) to function.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us
